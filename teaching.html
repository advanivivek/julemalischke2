Workshops for Guitar, Singing and Songwriting by Jule Malischke and Cristin Claas



You are a singer, a guitar player or maybe both?
Whatever level you are now, we will help you to improve your skills, individually!


What is it all about?
- Stimulate the urge to explore new ways to discover your musical talents
- Exercise your vocal abilities in a way that feels good, help you to find modern and varied chords,
grooves and accompaniment skills
- Synchronization of guitar and vocals
- Creative work on song structure
- Freedom for your own creative ideas and opportunities to make music together


For whom?
Anyone between ages 15 to 99, who likes to make music and who likes to sing and would like to live  the magic of doing these things together 


Where does this take place?
In various magical places, could be in Northern Germany, the Pfalz, in Sweden. Always in a serene,
stimulating and peaceful setting
